Our Farm

The farm has been owned by the Bouzid family since early 1920’s in a region called Wlad Abdulla in Taroudant, Southern Morocco. The farm sits on 9 acres and is run by many sons, daughters and grandchildren. The region’s only income comes from these organic oils and products and live stock.

The men and women harvest and make everything by hand.
