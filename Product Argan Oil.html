Argan Oil (culinary) is native to a region in Southern Morocco and can’t be found anywhere else making it one the rarest and most expensive oils in the world. Argan oil is rich is vitamin E ( 3 times more than Olive oil), the fatty acids in Argan Oul can help boost your levels of “good”cholesterol and lower “bad” cholesterol, potentially reducing your risk of heart disease.Argan kernels are roasted giving the oil a nutty flavor. It can be drizzled over salads, roasted,grilled veggies and meats and a lot more
